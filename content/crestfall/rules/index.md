Keep it simple: **be respectful, play fair, and have fun.** If something feels wrong, it probably is.

<div class="rule-list">

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="prohibit") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>No Griefing</strong>
<p>Don't destroy, modify, or damage other players' builds without their permission. This includes setting fires, flooding, placing blocks on someone else's build, or anything that ruins their work.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="lock") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>No Stealing</strong>
<p>Don't take items from chests, farms, or builds that aren't yours — unless the owner has clearly marked them as public or given you permission.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="hand") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>No Harassment</strong>
<p>Treat everyone with respect. Insults, slurs, targeted bullying, threats, and any form of discrimination are not tolerated — in chat or voice.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="shield-warning") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>No Cheating or Exploiting</strong>
<p>No hacked clients, x-ray, duping, or abusing game exploits. Mods that give an unfair advantage are not allowed. Cosmetic and performance mods are fine.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="sword") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>No Spawn Killing</strong>
<p>Don't repeatedly kill players at spawn or trap them in ways they can't escape. PvP is fine if both players agree to it.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="eye") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>Keep It Appropriate</strong>
<p>No offensive builds, skins, or usernames. Use common sense — this is a shared space.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="rule card">
<span class="rule-icon">{{ icon(name="lightning") }}</span>
<div class="rule-body">
<strong>Don't Lag the Server</strong>
<p>Avoid building massive redstone contraptions, entity farms, or anything that causes significant server lag. If in doubt, ask first.</p>
</div>
</div>

</div>

{% alert(icon="info", title="Got griefed or robbed?", color="blue") %}
Don't panic — **backups are taken constantly**. [Contact me](@/socials/index.md) as soon as it happens and I'll revert what's needed. The faster you report it, the less data other players lose from a rollback.
{% end %}

{% alert(icon="warning", title="Enforcement", color="yellow") %}
Breaking the rules may result in a **warning**, **temporary ban**, or **permanent ban** depending on the severity. Serious offences like griefing or stealing will be dealt with immediately. If you witness something, report it in the [Matrix room](https://matrix.to/#/#crestfall:mambuco.dev/) or [contact me directly](@/socials/index.md).
{% end %}
